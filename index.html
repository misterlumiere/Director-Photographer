Greetings, Internet.

Chris Rusch is an Austin-based director & producer with a focus on dramatic and visual arts. His short film and live-video works have screened at SXSW and on the PBS show "The Frontier." His most recent visual work can be seen with the ambient mixed-media duo Twigs & Yarn. Chris is currently working on his debut feature film titled "Dirty Hands."
